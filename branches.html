Wellipanna

This is our first restaurant outside Colombo suburbs. But still this is located in the highway exit where people can relax amidst their journey. Here also we offer breakfast, lunch and dinner. We have wide range of fried rice set menus, lamp rice, buriyani menus noodles and vast range of curry and devilled dishes for take away. Not only these Chinese dishes but also we have Sri Lankan dishes like kotththu and rice and curry for you to dine in , take away or getting delivered. We can accomadate. We have private party halls and famiely rooms. All our function halls are – upto 100 pax
